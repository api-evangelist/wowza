Quick Answer Modern transportation agencies stream live traffic camera video to two very different audiences in parallel. Traffic management center operators need sub-second latency and secure delivery, while the public needs broad reach across 511 sites and travel apps. The architecture that supports both at scale separates contribution from delivery.
