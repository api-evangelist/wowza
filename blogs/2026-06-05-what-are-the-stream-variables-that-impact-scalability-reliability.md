The article identifies five key stream variables — resolution, frame rate, codec choice, bitrate ladder, and protocol — that determine compute load on video streaming servers. Each variable taxes specific hardware resources, and understanding their impact helps optimize capacity planning and prevent bottlenecks in live streaming deployments.
